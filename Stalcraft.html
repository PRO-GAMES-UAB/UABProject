STALCRAFT is a dynamic MMOFPS with survival horror elements, set in the open world of the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone.

ACERCA DE ESTE JUEGO

A unique combination of MMO and FPS STALCRAFT is a fresh and progressive adaptation of a familiar setting in the format of a massively multiplayer online first—person shooter. Successfully combining the complete freedom of PvP and interesting PvE elements, the game allows you to plunge into the mysterious and dangerous world of Chernobyl Exclusion Zone not only alone, but also together with your friends. Complete a variety of tasks, search for mysterious artifacts in deadly anomalies, pick up equipment according to your playstyle, join the showdown between the factions or even engage in peaceful trading on bases. Choose your own path, stalker!
